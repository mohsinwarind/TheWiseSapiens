Rules

General Rules

1-Entries must be original, taken by the participant, and not previously published or awarded

2-Photographs should not promote or glorify harmful or illegal activities.

3-By entering, participants grant permission for their images to be used for promotional purposes.

4-Must include a brief description (70 words max) explaining concept and inspiration.

5-Participants can submit a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 Photographs.

6-You can use any graphics or cartoons in your images to enhance creativity


Category-Specific Rules


Nature

1-There should be landscapes, plants, animals or natural scenery in the photos taken

2-No human elements or man-made structures.

3-Minimal processing; no excessive manipulation.


Portrait

1-Focus on capturing people and emotions.

2-Can include individuals or groups.

3-Must be taken with consent from subjects.

4-Minimal processing; no excessive manipulation.


Urban

1-Showcase urban life, architecture, and character.

2-Including humans or anything else is upto you.

3-Must be taken in public spaces.


Abstract

1-Focus on unique angles, colors, and compositions.

2-Can use any subject matter.

3-Encourages creative processing and manipulation.

Submission Rules:

1- Digital Images Only (Like in JPEG or PNG format)

2- Maximum file size: 10MB.

3- No watermarks or logos.

4- Submit the form before deadline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the Capture Quest?
The event is open to anyone interested in photography, regardless of skill level or experience.
What are the categories for submission?
The categories are Nature, Portrait Photography, Street and Urban Photography, and Abstract Photography.
How many photos can I submit?
Participants may submit 3 to 5 entries per category.
What file formats are accepted for submissions?
JPEG and PNG formats are accepted with suitable resolution.
Are there any restrictions on photo editing?
Minor edits are allowed, but excessive manipulation that alters the original image significantly is not permitted.
Do I need permission to photograph people for portraits?
Yes, you must obtain consent from individuals featured in your portrait photos before submitting.
How will my photos be judged?
Photos will be evaluated based on creativity, technical skill, adherence to category themes, and overall impact.
Will participants receive a certificate?
Yes, all participants will receive a certificate within 15 days of the event, along with a shoutout on our website and social media platforms!
Can I resubmit my submitted pictures?
Yes, you can resubmit your pictures by contacting the organizers before the submission deadline.
Is there an entry fee for participating?
No, participation in Capture Quest is completely free of cost!
What kind of description should I write with my photograph?
Include a brief description or caption that explains your work and its significance in less than 70 words.
Can I submit photos taken before the contest announcement?
Yes, if the photos are original works created by you and fit within the specified categories.
What if I have technical issues during submission?
If you encounter any technical difficulties, please contact us for assistance ASAP! :)
How can I ensure my photos meet the required resolution standards?
Check dimensions, use a good camera, edit if needed, avoid digital zoom, and preview the image. GoodLuck !
