About the Contest

Welcome , poetic humans, to the inaugural of Poetry Conquest-I by The Wise Sapiens!

In a world that rarely slows down, we invite you to pause and find meaning through poetry.

The Poet's Code

Universal Principles

  1. Your poems must be original compositions .
  2. Each verse should honor the dignity of life; avoid any kind of content that glorifies or instigate violence or be disrepectfull.
  3. By sharing your work, you grant us permission to feature selected poems in our anthology and platforms.
  4. Include a brief context (70 words maximum) revealing your inspiration or intent to write that specific poem.
  5. You may submit atleast 1 and maximum 5 poems in total across both languages.
  6. Visual elements that enhance your poetic expression are welcomed.
  7. Beware! AI-Generated Poems are highly discouraged and can leads to permanent disqualification.

English Poetry

  • You may submit poems in any form - sonnet, free verse, narrative, lyrical, or haiku.
  • Maximum length: 50 lines per poem.
  • It’s up to you to follow the rhythm or let your creative self unfold.
  • All poems in the English category must be primarily in English, though occasional phrases in other languages are welcome between your verses.

Urdu Poetry

  • All traditional forms are welcome - ghazal, nazm, qasida, or modern experimentation.
  • Maximum length: 50 lines per poem.
  • Poems should use the Urdu language, written in Nastaliq script however for some exceptional case, the occasional phrases in other languages , for instance persion, are welcome between your verses.

Submission Guidelines

  • Digital text only (DOC, DOCX, or PDF format).
  • Maximum file size: 5MB.
  • No watermarks or signatures within the poems.
  • All submissions must arrive before the closing date.

Your Rewards

  • Recognition on our platforms and an internationally verifiable certificate.
  • All participants receive certificates acknowledging their artistic journey.
  • Get featured on our platform, along with a shout-out.
  • The invaluable experience of sharing your voice in a community of poets.
  • Exposure to a wider audience who are seeking meaning through your verse.
  • Connection with fellow wordsmiths , literary custodians, and Poets.
  • Thoughtful feedback from experienced judges to nurture your craft.
  • A meaningful addition to your creative portfolio, also get a long-term support from The Wise Sapiens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join this event?
Our journey welcomes all who love language—from noob to master poets. There are no barriers of experience or credential for this event.
Can I submit in both English and Urdu?
Yes, you may submit poems in both languages, but please ensure you follow the specific guidelines for each language category.
How many poems may I contribute?
You may offer between 1 and 5 poems in total, distributed across both language categories as you wish.
In what format can I upload the poem file?
You can submit poems in DOC, DOCX, and PDF formats, which allow your formatting and visual elements to remain intact.
Do I need translate my Urdu poetry?
No , not at all ! You can submit your Urdu poetry in its original form.
Can I add images or any other visual elements along with my poem?
Yes, illustrations or visual companions that enrich your poetic expression are welcome additions to your submission however keep in mind that they are not mandatory.
By what measure will poems be contemplated?
Our judges consider creativity, critical and technical craft, adherence to tradition (where applicable), emotional resonance, and originality of voice.
Will all participants receive recognition?
Yes, all participants will receive a certificate within 15 days after the ending date, along with acknowledgment across our platforms.
May I revise poems after sending them forth?
Yes, you may submit revised versions by contacting the guides before the submission deadline closes.
Is any fee required to join this gathering?
No, the Poetry Conquest or The Wise Sapiens requires no financial offering. Participation is entirely free.
What is required in the context of the poem?
Include a brief note (under 70 words) revealing your inspiration, context, or intent behind each work.
Can you feature the best-category winner in a reel , letting them narrate their poem?
Yes, if their is any possibility of featuring the winner and their poem, we will definitely don't want to miss that chance.
What if I encounter difficulties on this path?
Don't worry , you have us on our ends, you can contact us on Email or contact number if you had any query at all.
